About Us

 

Nehru Arts Science College, Kanhangad was established in the year 1966, Nehru Arts Science College, Kanhangad is affiliated to Kannur University, Kannur.

                 Nehru Arts & Science College, Kanhangad is a premier institution of Kasargode District. Students of the college have excelled in academic and extra-curricular activities. Apart from securing ranks at the university level examinations over the years, the students have performed outstandingly well in sports, games and fine arts competitions."The Nehru Memorial Education Society” under the distinguished President ship of Late Shri.C.K.Nair.The foundation Stone of the college building was laid on 1stApril 1968.The first year Pre-Degree Classes commenced on 14thAugust 1968.The College was upgraded in 1972. Now the college is affiliated to Kannur University.
